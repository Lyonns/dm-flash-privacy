Privacy Policy for DM Flash Chrome Extension

Last updated: July 8, 2025

Overview

DM Flash is a Chrome extension that helps users generate personalized messages for professional networking on platforms like LinkedIn. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  • Business Information: Your business description, website URL, and name for message personalization
  • Message Preferences: Tone, length, and other customization settings
  • Profile Data: We temporarily process publicly visible profile information from social platforms to generate personalized messages

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Statistics: Basic usage metrics to improve the extension (anonymized)
  • Error Logs: Technical error information to help us diagnose and fix issues (no personal data included)

How We Use Your Information

Message Generation

  • We send profile data and your preferences to OpenAI's API to generate personalized messages
  • Profile data is processed temporarily and not stored permanently on our servers
  • Generated messages are not saved, logged, or transmitted to our servers
  • All processing happens locally in your browser or via direct API calls to OpenAI

Extension Functionality

  • Settings and preferences are saved locally to personalize your experience
  • Usage tracking for subscription management
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers except for usage statistics

Data Sharing and Third Parties

OpenAI API

  • Profile data and message preferences are sent to OpenAI's API for message generation
  • This data is subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and data usage terms
  • We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy at https://openai.com/privacy/

No Data Sales or Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers, marketing companies, or data brokers
  • We only collect minimal usage data for service operation

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's encrypted storage
  • Usage data is stored locally for subscription management
  • Data is encrypted using Chrome's built-in security mechanisms

Data Retention

  • Settings persist until you remove them or uninstall the extension
  • Profile data is processed temporarily during message generation and immediately discarded
  • Generated messages are not stored by the extension

User Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • You can view, modify, or delete your stored settings at any time through the extension popup
  • You can clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • You can opt out of usage analytics in the extension settings

Subscription and Payment

DM Flash operates on a freemium model with usage limits. Payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers and we do not store payment information.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or our website.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@dmflash.com
Website: https://dmflash.com

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

This extension uses OpenAI's API for message generation. Your use of this extension is also subject to:

  • OpenAI's Terms of Use: https://openai.com/terms/
  • OpenAI's Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/

Social Media Platforms

This extension interacts with social media platforms (LinkedIn, etc.) to read publicly available profile information. Your use is subject to those platforms' terms of service.

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the DM Flash Chrome extension.
